Bike tour Berlin

Since my brother and I belong more to the sporty guys, we thought, why shouldn't we explore Berlin by bike. So we grabbed a rental bike and went for a spin through Berlin. Since I personally haven't created a jupyter notebook for a while now, I think this is a good opportunity.

What is planed:

A map with all the routes we have taken this will include the following Python elements:

  • Jupyter Notebook
  • Pandas
  • Basemap
  • Matplotlib

The notebook is unfortunately not quite finished yet and will follow.

All in all we were really quite sporty and cycled a good 100 kilometers across Berlin, here are a few impressions from Berlin,
